About

TaftLivin' is a web application designed to help students and young professionals find the best accommodations around the De La Salle University (DLSU) area. Whether you're searching for a condo, dorm, or apartment, TaftLivin' offers a reliable platform where users can browse, review, and share honest opinions about various establishments. Our mission is to make the search for the perfect living space easier by providing detailed ratings, real reviews, and helpful insights from fellow students and tenants.
