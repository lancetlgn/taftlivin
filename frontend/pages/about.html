About TaftLivin'

In the busy environment in the heart of Taft Avenue, most DLSU Manila students may opt to stay in nearby condominiums or dorms to balance their academic responsibilities with convenience and avoid commuting through Manila's harsh traffic. However, choosing the right accommodations can be a daunting task due to the myriad of options and varying living/budget conditions. TaftLivin' aims to fill this gap by offering a centralized platform where students can share their detailed experiences about dorm life in a more accessible way.

Our web application, "DLSU Rate My Dorm," empowers students by providing detailed insights into dorm life and enhancing the overall student living experience. With features like user accounts, interactive dorm maps, search and filter functionality, and community discussion boards, TaftLivin' is your go-to platform for making informed decisions about accommodations near DLSU.
